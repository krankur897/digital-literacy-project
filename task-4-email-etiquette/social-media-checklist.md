# 📱 Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## 📌 Introduction
Nowadays, social media is a big part of students' everyday life. We use it for chatting, sharing ideas, learning new things, and entertainment. However, if not used carefully, it can create problems like privacy issues, cyberbullying, and damage to reputation.

So, it is important for students to use social media in a responsible way. Below are some simple do's and don'ts every college student should follow.

---

## ✅ Do's for College Students

### 1. Be Careful About Privacy
- Check your privacy settings regularly  
- Make sure personal information is visible only to trusted people  

### 2. Check Information Before Sharing
- Verify facts before posting or forwarding  
- Avoid spreading misinformation  

### 3. Use Social Media for Growth
- Share your skills, projects, and achievements  
- Build a positive digital presence  

### 4. Think Before You Post
- Remember: what goes online can stay forever  
- Always review before posting  

### 5. Be Respectful Online
- Use polite language  
- Treat others with respect  

---

## ❌ Don'ts for College Students

### 1. Do Not Get Involved in Cyberbullying
- Avoid hurtful comments  
- Do not spread hate or make fun of others  

### 2. Avoid Sharing Personal Details
- Never share phone numbers, addresses, passwords, or bank details  

### 3. Do Not Copy Others' Work
- Avoid plagiarism  
- Always give proper credit  

### 4. Avoid Posting Negative Content
- Do not post offensive content about college, teachers, or friends  
- Maintain a positive online image  

### 5. Do Not Click on Unknown Links
- Avoid suspicious links  
- Protect yourself from scams and malware  

---

## 🎯 Conclusion
Social media is helpful when used correctly. By following these do's and don'ts, students can stay safe, build a positive image, and avoid unnecessary risks.

👉 Always remember: responsible use of social media plays a big role in shaping your personal and professional life.
